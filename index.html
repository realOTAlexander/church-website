BAPTISM AND CONFIRMATION

Our church views baptism as a sacred ordinance symbolizing a new beginning and a commitment to follow Jesus Christ. Typically performed by immersion in water, baptism signifies a cleansing from past sins and a spiritual rebirth. Immediately following baptism, confirmation occurs with the laying on of hands by our priest. This act is seen as the conferral of the Holy Ghost, a divine gift meant to guide, comfort, and strengthen the individual throughout their life's journey.
